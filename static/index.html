Coduno lets you assess technical abilities and skills of potential software developers without time consuming meetings. We take care of your technical interview and give an overview of how candidates performed.

So that you can hire the best.

Fingerprint

Fingerprint is our way to check candidates. At it's core, Fingerprint is a coding challenge that takes less than two hours, but generates vast amount of data to mine. It goes beyond synthetic tests and asks for real software engineering.

We soak up the code of your candidates and pipe it to our high end engine. In the process of executing and analyzing, we collect various metrics, such as code smells, execution time, use of best practices, ...

On top of that, the assessment is tailored to the very specific position of the candidate within your company.
